Teach to Achieve

If you want to master a concept, teach it!

Teach to Achieve is a classroom tool designed to help students master concepts through teaching.

How does Teach to Achieve work?
Teachers create a classroom and post an assignment with instructions for students.
Students join the classroom and upload a video, document, or other mediums, explaining a concept in order to prepare for exams or quizzes.

A CS50 Project by Shania Hurtado & Emily Figueiredo
